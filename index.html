Procedure to get Lenovo Driver Update on Windows 10 or 11


You may find it interesting that you may get "lenovo driver update" either of the 2 ways -


(A) Via Windows Update



  • Tap 'Start'

  • Under Settings, Go to 'Update & Security'

  • Then click 'Windows Update'

  • Click 'Check for updates'.

  • You click Download button if any "lenovo driver update" available.

  • This makes download and installation automated.

(B)Via Device Manager


  • Firstly press Windows + X and click Device Manager .

  • In the device category -> expand Display drivers -> Right click Lenovo graphics card -> Update driver

  • Opt "Search automatically for updated driver software".

  • Get "lenovo driver update"s found,downloaded and installed.
